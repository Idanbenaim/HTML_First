Meet the Family

Ellie is our 1.5 years old girl. She is very active and wants to do anything her bigger siblings are doing. She loves food and to be read books. Ellie is always trying to catch up with her brothers and sisters, she is a very curious and playful little girl.

Ketem is our dog, he is a big part of our family and we love him. He is always there to wag his tail and give us cuddles when we need it. He is always up for a walk and a game of fetch. He is a great companion and friend.
